The Problem We Solve

Municipal services in Nepal today vs. with Municipal OS

🚫 Today's Reality

  • Citizens queue for hours at ward offices to submit paperwork
  • No visibility into application status — repeated follow-up visits
  • Manual routing of files between departments, easy to lose
  • Officers lack accountability — no record of who did what, when
  • Certificate/document production is manual and error-prone
  • No data to measure service delivery performance
  • Each municipality works differently — no standardization

✅ With Municipal OS

  • Citizens apply online anytime, from any device
  • Real-time status tracking at every workflow step
  • Automated digital routing — no paperwork lost
  • Full audit trail: every action logged with timestamps
  • Auto-generated, template-driven official PDF certificates
  • SLA dashboard shows on-time delivery rates to leadership
  • Configurable per municipality — one platform, many offices

User Roles & Capabilities

Four distinct roles with scoped access and features

👤

Citizen

Any resident of a municipality. Self-registers and uses the portal to access government services.

  • Register and log in to the citizen portal
  • Browse available services for their municipality
  • Submit new service applications with document uploads
  • Track application status and workflow progress in real-time
  • View step-by-step history with timestamps
  • Respond to document requests from officers
  • Download official approved PDF certificates
  • Receive in-app notifications on status changes
  • Use interface in English or Nepali
🏢

Ward Officer

Ward-level municipal staff who process applications that reach their designated workflow steps.

  • View the municipality-wide pending applications queue
  • See personally assigned applications dashboard
  • Pick up available steps matching their role
  • Be assigned steps by supervisors
  • Review citizen documents attached to applications
  • Approve or reject application steps with notes
  • Request additional documents from citizens
  • Edit certificate merge fields before finalization
  • Preview generated certificate PDF before finalizing
  • Finalize official certificates for citizen download
🏛️

Municipal Officer

Senior staff who handle higher-level workflow steps and have broader visibility.

  • All Ward Officer capabilities
  • Assigned to higher-authority workflow steps
  • Full municipality-wide application visibility
  • Access to completed applications list
  • Certificate preview, editing, and finalization
  • Re-provision generated documents if templates updated
⚙️

Administrator

Municipality or system administrator who configures services, workflows, templates, and monitors performance.

  • Create and manage service types with required documents
  • Design and update multi-step workflows per service
  • Set SLA targets at service and step level
  • Upload and version DOCX certificate templates
  • Configure document generation per service type
  • Activate/deactivate template versions
  • View SLA performance dashboard with charts
  • Drill down per-application SLA report with filters
  • Query full audit log with user/time filters
  • Manage municipality settings and officers
  • View all applications across the municipality

📊 Application Status States

  • Submitted — initial state, awaiting first step pickup
  • In Progress — currently being processed by an officer
  • Documents Requested — citizen must upload more files
  • Approved — all steps completed; triggers cert generation
  • Rejected — declined with reason; citizen notified

🔢 Per-Step Runtime States

  • Pending — waiting for previous steps to complete
  • Waiting to be Picked — available for officer pickup
  • In Progress — assigned to and being worked by an officer
  • Completed — step passed; moves to next step
  • Skipped — bypassed (future branching feature)

Automated Certificate Generation

DOCX template upload, auto field extraction, versioning with activation, merge field editing, background PDF rendering, preview/finalize workflow, citizen download endpoint.

SLA Tracking & Performance Dashboard

Built

Nepal business-time SLA calculator, per-step and per-application due dates, admin SLA dashboard with charts and drill-down, on-time % metrics by service and officer.

Enhanced SLA Visibility Dashboard

Planned

Breakdown by ward, officer-level on-time rates, exportable reports for leadership, public-facing transparency pages showing aggregate service delivery rates.

Officer Digital Signature Hook

Planned

Signature image or digital signing step embedded in the certificate finalization flow. Keeps a clear chain from officer identity to final document.

SMS / Email Notifications

Planned

Push citizens outside the portal via SMS or email when their application status changes — critical for citizens without daily portal access.

Conditional Workflow Branching

Future

Policy-driven DAG workflows: IF/ELSE rules skip or add steps at runtime based on application data (e.g. large builds need engineer approval). Full rule audit logging.

Event Bus & Real-Time Notifications

Future

RabbitMQ integration (already in tech stack plan) for async event-driven architecture, WebSocket real-time updates to officer queues and citizen dashboards.

Public Transparency Portal

Future

Read-only public view showing service availability, average processing times, and aggregate SLA compliance — supporting open government initiatives.
